Flickering App

The background flickers according to the texts you write in the fields below. The initializing pattern is repeated as long until a timeout. After that, the starting-pattern will be transmitted one time. Only "0" and "1" are allowed of both of them.

After the start pattern, the text will be transmitted. Each character will be represented as the first 8 bits for the utf-16 codepoint of that charachter.

Tab on the background to hide/show controls.
